Bienvenidos a nuestra mesa

The comal is the heart of the kitchen

A comal is the flat griddle Mexican cooks have used for generations to warm tortillas and roast chiles until they blister. It is where flavor begins, and it is where our name comes from.

We are dedicated to bringing the rich and diverse flavors of Mexican cuisine to your table. Our menu showcases the best of Mexico's regional dishes, prepared with authentic ingredients and traditional cooking techniques, from sizzling fajitas and enchiladas to tamales and guacamole. Every dish is made from scratch.

Featured plates

A handful of the dishes our regulars come back for. The full menu runs deep.

Al Pastor

Chile marinated pork with sweet grilled pineapple, onion and cilantro, served hot off the fire.

Carne Asada

Grilled steak cooked over the fire, served with rice, beans and warm handmade tortillas.

Street Tacos

Your choice of meat on warm corn tortillas with fresh onion, cilantro and house salsa.

Birria de Res

Slow braised beef in guajillo and chile, dipped and crisped, served with a cup of rich consomme.

Coctel de Camaron

Chilled shrimp in a bright tomato broth with avocado, onion, cilantro and lime.

Molcajete El Comal

A volcanic stone bowl bubbling with steak, chicken, shrimp, chorizo, nopal and grilled onion.
